# Case Studies

Each case study below is drawn from real work. Names, companies, and specific identifying details are removed; the problems, decisions, and outcomes are real.

I write these in a consistent structure so they&rsquo;re easy to skim:

- **Context** — the org and the constraints
- **Problem** — what was actually broken
- **Approach** — what I tried, what I rejected, why
- **Outcome** — what changed, what didn&rsquo;t, what I&rsquo;d do differently

## The case studies

- **[Driving content governance without authority](./governance-without-authority)** — A KM lead with no direct reports tries to introduce governance at an org without leadership sponsorship. What works, what doesn&rsquo;t.
- **[Rebuilding a Guru instance for actual use](./guru-instance-rebuild)** — Inheriting a KM tool that everyone has access to but no one trusts. The IA, governance, and behavior-change work to fix it.
