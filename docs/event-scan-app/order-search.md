# Order Search

Use the Search tool to locate scan data for customers, attendees, orders, IDs, and barcodes. After finding an order, you can view the current scan status and manually check tickets in or out of your event.

<img src={require('./img/searchselectededit.png').default} alt="Select Events" width="300" />

The Search page opens. Search for an order using any of the following:

- Customer Name
- Name on Ticket / Attendee Name
- Confirmation Number
- Sale ID
- Barcode

To check in all tickets in an order:

1. Tap the order to view all tickets individually.
2. Tap the tickets to check in.
3. Tap **Check In All**.

To check in specific tickets, tap **Check In** on the individual ticket.

Validated tickets display a **Check In** status. Tap **Check Out** to check the ticket out.

## View Scan History

The Scan History page lets you review recent check-ins and check-outs for your synced events. Use it to verify previous scans, troubleshoot entry issues, or confirm a customer's check-in status.

- **Access**: Open Scan History from the Scan screen or the Search screen.
- **Data**: All scans (check-ins and check-outs) appear in chronological order.
- **Scope**: Only scans from events synced to your device appear.

### Accessing Scan History from the Search Screen

1. From the bottom menu, tap the **Search** tab to open the Orders/Customers page.
2. Tap the **History** icon in the top-right corner.
3. The Scan History page displays a list of recent scans, showing order or ticket details, check-in or check-out status, and the date and time of each scan.

## Configure Events

To change which events are loaded, tap **Select Synced Events**. Select any additional events and tap **Confirm**.

To remove events from your device, tap **Unload Events**.
