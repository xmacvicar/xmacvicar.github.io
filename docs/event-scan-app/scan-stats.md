# Scan Stats

Need to decide whether to hold the show for latecomers? See how many tickets are outstanding, both as a total number and as a percentage of tickets sold.

Pull down from the top of the page to force your device to sync all scan information with the system. With a reliable Wi-Fi connection, the scanner syncs periodically on its own. You do not need to initiate syncs manually.

## Available Stats

- **Tickets Checked In**: The number of tickets scanned at all selected events as of the last sync.
- **Total Outstanding Tickets**: Tickets not yet scanned into the event.
- **Waiting to Upload**: Tickets scanned on this device that have not yet synced with the database.
- **Last Sync**: The date and time of the most recent successful sync.
